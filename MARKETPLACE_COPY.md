## Full description

Calendar Heatmap is a custom Power BI visual that presents daily values in a month-by-month calendar layout.

It is designed for report authors who want to highlight daily patterns in a format that is immediately familiar to business users. Instead of scanning dense tables or generic charts, users can review activity by day across one or more months and quickly spot trends, gaps, peaks, and operational cycles.

The visual supports both day-level and month-level interactions, allowing report consumers to click a specific day or month and cross-filter the rest of the report. It also supports flexible formatting so authors can adapt the visual to different dashboard styles.

Main capabilities:

- calendar heatmap by month
- day and month cross-filtering
- editable title and subtitle
- dynamic header text fields
- Power BI `fx` color formatting
- responsive visual layout
- presentation-ready styling

Typical scenarios:

- sales and revenue tracking
- attendance and staffing review
- support or ticket monitoring
- operational activity analysis
- production and throughput reporting
